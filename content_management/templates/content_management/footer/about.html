About Spotted Stockholm

In the dynamic year of 2022, amid Stockholm's vibrant streets, Spotted emerged as the ultimate guide to the city's hidden gems. As the world witnessed significant events, from cultural milestones to technological breakthroughs, we quietly set out to redefine how Stockholmers explore their city.

our inception, Spotted has been synonymous with curated content and insider recommendations, earning the trust of Stockholm's diverse population. With a monthly audience of over half a million urban adventurers and culture enthusiasts, we've become the go-to source for discovering Stockholm's best-kept secrets.

In addition to our comprehensive guide, we introduced Spotted Memberships, granting exclusive access to curated experiences and discounts of up to 50% off. From VIP events to secret gatherings, our members enjoy unparalleled privileges across Stockholm.

Today, we are a passionate team of Stockholm enthusiasts dedicated to crafting accessible and actionable content. Our goal is simple: to inspire people to embrace the vibrant spirit of Stockholm and create unforgettable memories in the city we love.
